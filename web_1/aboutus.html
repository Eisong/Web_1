About

T@-TRACK SECURE POINT cow tracker is designed to recover thousands of stolen cows, resolve cattle ownership disputes, as well as drastically reduce or eliminate unnecessary killing of Ranchers.

T@-TRACK SECURE POINT: This project was inspired by concern, fear and effect of farmers-herdsmen conflicts, theft and land dispute in my area 2018. The year 2018 was the climate of herdsmen-farmer conflicts. During this event I also viewed a lot of theft activities. I literally watch war cost by land dispute on cattle encroachment, destroying of farm produces by cow’s. Well economy derangement were lot of things were destroy.
So I thought of a solution. Cow Tracking Systems

Cow theft, land conflict is a recurrent and singular phenomenon that is observed in many African countries. In recent years, it is increasingly the main concern of farmers. It is a typically regional phenomenon, but according to the latest observations in scale of 1-10, it tends to become sub-regional. It is, for African countries, one of the major constraints to livestock development. Indeed the problems posed by this scourge affect both farmers and government. Its impact has a social, safe, and economical dimension Concern is to keep better track of your cows location, health, and if they out of range? Outfitting a sensor and tracking device to a cow’s collar, ear, leg, tail, or via an ingestible microchip gives farmers the ability to track a cow’s activity levels, and other key behaviors like grazing activity to increase output and overall herd wellness.

Key Benefits:
1. Easy animal Identification
2. Calving detection so the new calf can be entered into the system and so live births can be monitored
3. GPS location tracking to monitor herd
4. Integrate with farm database tracking of any medications or treatments and output
5. Rumination tracking to see how long they have been eating to monitor health and manage overall herd feed rations

Key Drivers
The main drive for the project is the cows theft and herdsmen and farmer conflict
